# Privacy Policy — Discount Calculate

**Last updated:** May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application **Discount Calculate** (also known as "Global Discount Calculator", hereinafter "the App") handles data when you use it.

## 1. Developer

**Developer:** Andymarcial  
**Contact:** andymartin1991@gmail.com

## 2. Data Collected by the App

The App is a simple discount and savings calculator. It does **not** require a user account, does **not** collect personal information, and does **not** send any data to the developer's own servers.

The App does not store any user data beyond the current session (the values you enter in the price and discount fields are not saved after you close the App).

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**.

- **Data collected by AdMob:** AdMob may collect your Advertising ID (GAID on Android), device information (model, OS version), IP address, and other identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
- **Purpose:** to display ads and fund the free availability of the App.
- **Privacy policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **AdMob data use:** [https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting the ad settings on your device:
- Android: **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

### 3.2 Google Play Services

The App runs on Android devices that use **Google Play Services**.

- **Privacy policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 4. App Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

- **Internet (`android.permission.INTERNET`):** Required to load advertisements from AdMob.
- **Ad ID (`com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID`):** Required by AdMob to serve ads. On Android 13+, this permission is requested at runtime if the user has not limited ad tracking.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take the necessary action.

## 6. Data Security

The App itself does not transmit personal data to any server controlled by the developer. All communication with third-party services (AdMob) is handled by Google's own SDKs using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates the current version. We advise you to review this page periodically.
